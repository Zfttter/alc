Classification and production methods of Chinese pasta
Production method
The production method of Chinese Lamian Noodles emphasizes the skill of drawing and the feeling of the dough's stretch
Mix flour and water thoroughly to form a soft and elastic dough, laying a foundation for subsequent production.
By kneading, the gluten of the dough is fully formed, giving it elasticity and extensibility
Adjust the shape and structure of the dough through "sliding" to make it suitable for drawing.
By stretching and folding, the noodles are pulled to the required thickness and length to complete the production of Lamian Noodles.
The essence of Fried Rice with eggs lies in the selection of rice and the skill of cooking
Choose overnight rice, which has less moisture and is easy to stir fry with distinct grains. Crush the rice with a spoon or hand to make it loose. Prepare seasonings such as eggs, scallions, salt, pepper, cooking oil, etc
Crack the eggs into a bowl, add an appropriate amount of salt and pepper, and stir well. Pour an appropriate amount of cooking oil into the pot, heat it up, pour in the egg mixture, stir quickly with a spatula, fry the eggs into small pieces, and set aside.
Add an appropriate amount of cooking oil to the pot, heat it up, then add chopped scallions and stir fry until fragrant. Pour the scattered rice into the pot and stir fry evenly with a spatula to ensure even heating of the rice. Add an appropriate amount of salt and pepper, season evenly
Pour the scrambled eggs into the rice and continue stir frying evenly until the eggs and rice are fully mixed
The essence of dumplings lies in the techniques of filling and wrapping
Choose fresh meat, vegetables, and seasonings. Chop the meat and finely chop the vegetables.
Put chopped meat and vegetable powder into a large bowl, add an appropriate amount of salt, soy sauce, cooking wine, chopped scallions and ginger, sesame oil and other seasonings, stir evenly
Place the dumpling wrapper in your hand, add an appropriate amount of filling, fold the dumpling wrapper in half, and pinch the edges tightly with your hand to seal the dumpling.
Put the wrapped dumplings into boiling water and gently stir with a spoon to prevent them from sticking together
TThe essence of baozi lies in the softness of the dough and the deliciousness of the filling
Prepare flour, yeast, warm water, filling (such as meat filling, vegetable filling, etc.), seasonings such as scallions, ginger, garlic, etc
Pour the flour into a bowl, add yeast and warm water, and stir to make a dough
Add meat or vegetable filling to seasonings such as scallions, ginger, and garlic, and stir well
Wrap the filling into the dough and shape it into a bun
The essence of pies lies in their crispy outer skin and juicy and delicious filling
Prepare medium gluten flour, which can make the crust both tough and relatively soft. Generally, every 200 grams of flour is mixed with about 100-120 milliliters of warm water, and an additional 2-3 grams of salt are needed to increase the gluten of the flour.
There are many types of fillings, taking pork and scallion filling as an example. Select pork with alternating fat and lean, such as pork belly, and chop it up. Choose fresh scallions and cut them into scallions
Place the awakened dough on the cutting board, knead it into long strips, and then divide them into evenly sized small pieces. The size of each small dumpling is determined according to the desired size of the pie
Take the baked pie out of the pot and place it on a plate to let it dry for a while. The freshly cooked pie has a crispy crust and a steaming hot filling inside. It can be enjoyed together with soybean milk, millet Congee, etc. according to personal preference, and the taste is better
