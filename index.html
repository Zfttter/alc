History of Chinese Pasta
The history of noodles can be traced back to the Neolithic Age over 4000 years ago. Archaeologists have discovered a well preserved fossilized bowl of noodles made from millet and sorghum at the La Jia site in Qinghai, China.
With the development of agriculture, wheat was introduced to China around 2000 BC and gradually became an important grain crop in the north. People began to use stone mills to make flour from wheat, laying the foundation for the development of pasta.
